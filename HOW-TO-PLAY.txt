HOW I MET YOUR TOWER — How to play
==================================

You're the flight crew of an airliner that's falling apart. Fix the failing
systems, REACH THE TOWER (Air Traffic Control), and get talked down to a landing
before the clock — or the plane — runs out. Every flight is different.

INSTALL
- Double-click HowIMetYourTower.exe. That's it (it's self-contained). Windows
  may warn about an unknown publisher — click "More info" > "Run anyway".

CONTROLS
- WASD move · Shift sprint · Ctrl crouch · Space jump · Mouse look
- E interact (hold the look at a console and press E to work a task)
- C emote · V push-to-talk (voice) · B walkie-talkie · Esc pause
- Rebind everything in Settings > Controls.

SOLO
- Main menu > PLAY (SOLO) > walk to the glowing DEPARTURES desk > press E to board.

WITH FRIENDS (up to 4)
- One of you: MULTIPLAYER > HOST A FLIGHT. Tell the others your IP.
  (Same WiFi works out of the box; over the internet the host forwards UDP port
   24565, or use a tool like Hamachi/ZeroTier.)
- Everyone else: MULTIPLAYER > type the host's IP > JOIN.
- In the lobby: walk around, then READY UP. The host hits START FLIGHT.

THE FLIGHT
- A checklist of failures appears (top-left). Run to each console and press E
  repeatedly to work its steps. Split up — you can't be everywhere at once.
- FIX THE RADIO FIRST ("MEET THE TOWER") — that unlocks ATC guiding you down.
- Unfixed failures wreck your controls, drag, and hull integrity. Decompression
  and fires will eventually bring you down if ignored.
- Land inside the envelope (speed / descent / wings level / aligned) before T-0.

Good luck. Reach the Tower. Land alive.
